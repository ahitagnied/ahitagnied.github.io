Ahitagni Das

I'm an undergrad at Rice University, where I study ECE and CS. I currently work as a researcher at the Baraniuk Group.

Previously, I worked as a visiting research scentist at the Nano Cybernetik Biotrek, at the MIT Media Lab, where I studied microbatteries for autnomous sub-100 micron colloidal robots under the supervision of Dr. Deblina Sarkar. I have also worked on hexagonal Boron Nitride in sodium-ion batteries for EVs, and novel lithium-ion batteries from industrial waste at the Ajayan Group at Rice where I was advised by Dr. Pulickel Ajayan and Dr. Robert Vajtai. I also interned at the Nanofludics Lab at IIT Guwahati, where I was advised by Dr. Kalyan Raidongia on novel methods of harvesting energy from plastic waste.
